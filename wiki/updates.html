Weekly Update 10

Week of: March 24, 2020

Our team has revised and started implementing the front-end code for our website to ensure for easy use.

We are looking into HashID's for the URL, to help ensure you cant access a specific page when you are not allowed to

Our ESP32 is looking good, there was some changes to the way json data is saved but more successful testing has been done.

We have started revising this wiki, our SRS, and our video to meet end of month milestones.

The parsing of the "data" field in JSON has been parsed out and now be used into graphs

For the ESP32 board, severe serial bluetooth issues were fixed, resulting in successful operation/integration.

Due to the coronavirus spread, our team/school was apart of the transition period, reducing the amount of work done due to work from home only

Weekly Update 8

Week of: March 10, 2020

Our team has configured AWS/Azure services to host our DB and our API so the laravel application and wearable can access data.

Our ESP32 now utilises serial bluetooth, which is used for messaging/sending data over bluetooth

Weekly Update 7

Week of: March 3, 2020

The Coachable API has been altered to now accept proper JSON from the ESP32 hardware

Our laravel application can display proper data from DB using collections and blade.

Our team was investigating OTG and its implementation, but decided to scrap it. The ESP32 board has been tested more and we have added more data fields for JSON output.

Our team participated in the 4x4 event, reducing the amount of hours we contributed this week. However, it was a great experience for us to learn and have fun.
